About Nancy

Decades of Service.
One Purpose.

Dr. Nancy Young is a former Mayor of Tracy, longtime pastor, nonprofit leader, and community advocate with more than 30 years of service. Guided by faith and grounded in integrity, she has spent her career bringing people together to strengthen families and communities.

"As Tracy's first female and African American mayor, she championed initiatives that delivered over $300 million in community improvements."

Nancy is running for Governor as an Independent to restore unity, practical leadership, and opportunity for every Californian — regardless of party.

  • BA, University of California, Berkeley
  • MBA — business & organizational leadership
  • Th.D — theology & community ministry
  • 12 years serving in elected public office
  • CEO, The Young Connects nonprofit organization
  • Harvard Bloomberg Initiative recognition
  • Regional transportation planning leadership
  • Service on 13 external boards & commissions
  • Founder, Tracy Connects Day
  • Historic: 1st female & 1st African American Mayor of Tracy
